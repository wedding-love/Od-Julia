Our Concept

Our wedding is a meeting of two cultures — Russian and Danish. But above all, we are inspired by the Serbian tradition of celebrations, where the most important thing is people. Where you can sit at one long table for hours, talk, laugh, meet each other, enjoy delicious food, and simply be together.
This is exactly the atmosphere we want to create on this day. Without rush or a strict schedule — with the freedom to talk, raise toasts, share stories, dance, and enjoy time in great company.
We hope this evening will feel like a big summer dinner surrounded by our closest and dearest people: warm, heartfelt, and filled with genuine conversations, smiles, and new connections.
Your presence is the most precious gift to us. We would be deeply grateful for an envelope with warm wishes. For us, this day is about togetherness, love, and shared moments — and we can't wait to celebrate it with you.
See you soon!
With love
Od & Julia
