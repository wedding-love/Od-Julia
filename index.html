Dress Code

We want everyone to feel relaxed, beautiful, and comfortable throughout the evening.

That's why we decided not to impose a strict dress code or limit you to a specific color palette.

August in Belgrade is truly summer, so light fabrics, your favorite outfits, and anything that makes you feel great are perfect for the occasion.

For men, a smart casual style is an excellent choice.
For women, you can wear any summer dress, suit, or dressy outfit you like. The color and style are entirely up to you.

If you need a little inspiration, our wedding decor will feature soft shades of pink and green. But this is just part of the atmosphere we create, not a requirement.

And, of course, we hope to capture this special day in photographs that we will all enjoy looking back on for years to come, smiling as we recall this warm August evening.

Our Concept

Over the past few years, life has taken us to different countries. Some of us live in Russia, some in Denmark, some in Serbia, and some will be traveling to join us from other parts of the world.

That is why this day means so much more to us than just a wedding. It is a rare opportunity for all of us to come together. To hug the people we have missed, to introduce the people we love to one another, to sit at one table, talk about everything under the sun, laugh a lot, and simply spend time side by side.

During our time living in Serbia, we have truly come to love the local culture of celebration. Here, no one is in a hurry. People sit at the table for hours, give toasts, enjoy the food, talk, dance, and truly appreciate the moment itself.

This is exactly the atmosphere we want to preserve on the day of our Russian-Danish wedding. We want the evening to feel light and natural, without rushing, with time to talk freely, meet each other, laugh, dance, and simply enjoy being together.

We do not know when we will have the chance to gather everyone in this same constellation again. That is why we especially want to live this day slowly, to remember every meeting, every conversation, and every happy moment.

Thank you for being with us. Your presence is the most precious gift to us. If you would like to congratulate us with a gift as well, we would be grateful for an envelope with warm wishes.

See you soon!
With love
Julia & Od
